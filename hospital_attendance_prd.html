01 Overview & Goals CORE

HAMS is a production-grade attendance management system designed exclusively for hospitals and clinical environments. It replaces manual registers, biometric punch machines, and ad-hoc Excel sheets with a unified, tamper-proof digital platform.

The system enforces face recognition + GPS dual-factor verification on every check-in and check-out, preventing buddy punching, proxy attendance, and fake GPS submissions — the three most common fraud vectors in hospital settings.

Primary Objectives

1
Zero-fraud attendance capture
Face liveness + GPS geo-fence + device binding on every punch
2
Real-time staff visibility
Live dashboard showing who is working, on break, absent, or checked out at any moment
3
Automated rule enforcement
Late marks, half-day rules, overtime, break violations — all calculated automatically per shift
4
Payroll-ready exports
Monthly PDF and Excel reports with working hours, deductions, OT — ready for salary processing
5
Multi-department, multi-shift support
OPD, IPD, ICU, Pharmacy, Lab, OT — each department with its own shift and rule configuration
Scope: Android mobile app for staff + Responsive Web Admin Panel for HR/Management. Supports 20–1000+ employees. Multi-branch ready from day one.

05 Attendance Rules Engine LOGIC

Each employee inherits rules from their shift, overridable at the individual level. Rules run server-side on every punch.

RuleParameterExample
Grace PeriodMinutes after shift start before "Late" is markedDoctor: 20 min · Nurse: 10 min
Late MarkArrival after grace period09:21 on a 09:00 shift with 20 min grace → Late
Half Day RuleArrival threshold after which half day is markedArrive after 11:00 on morning shift → Half Day
Early LeaveCheck-out before shift endLeave before 16:45 on 17:00 shift → Early Leave
OvertimeMinutes after shift end before OT is countedWork 30+ min after shift end → Overtime
Break LimitTotal allowed break per dayLunch: 60 min · Tea: 15 min · Max total: 90 min
Geo-FenceRadius in meters from hospital centroidDefault: 100m. OT staff: same campus, different wing
Weekly OffDays exempted from attendanceSunday off, 2nd Saturday off

Working Hours Formula

Net Working Hours = (Check-Out Time − Check-In Time) − Total Break Duration

Late Minutes = Check-In Time − (Shift Start + Grace Period), if positive

Overtime Hours = Check-Out Time − Shift End, if > OT threshold

07 Break Management TRACKING

All breaks are face-verified at start. GPS optional on break end. Break durations tracked per type; violations auto-flagged.

Break TypeAllowed DurationFace on StartFace on EndViolation Rule
Lunch60 minYesYes> 75 min → Violation
Tea15 minYesYes> 20 min → Violation
Personal30 min/day totalYesYesExcess deducted from work hours
EmergencyAdmin-approvedYesOptionalTracked, no auto-violation
Break Violation Example: Lunch break allowed = 60 min. Employee took 95 min → Excess = 35 min. System flags as violation, deducts 35 min from working hours, manager notified via WhatsApp.
